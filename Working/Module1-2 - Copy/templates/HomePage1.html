TRANSPORTER'S USE

Everything you can do

Track Your Trucks

Track your delivery trucks anytime, anywhere with our real-time tracking system. Stay informed about your package's progress from departure to arrival, thanks to our user-friendly interface. Whether you're using a computer or a mobile device, monitoring your shipment is easy and convenient. You will be able to track all your Truck's locations at any time of your choosing.

Sign Up a new Truck Driver.

Since all the Truck Drivers are not literate. You may be required from their side to sign in to the system from their end. This way you can maintain a record of which truck drivers work under you. Plus, this allows you to sign up new truck drivers who may want to work under you.

Notifications

Your contractors will put up their own price to transport goods from one the pick up location to the destination. You can receive all the scheduled deliveries as notifications and then bid your amount to attract a customer to yourself. Make sure the offer is lucrative or the others might snatch your customer away from you. Moreover, you will be notified when the delivery is completed for your customer.

Bid your amount.

You can now bid your amount to the customer. Reminder: Be sure to make it cheaper and more convenient for the customer or you might end up losing the chance.

Maintain Your Record

You have probably dealt with dealing records on registers or on excel files. Now it's just one click away with your data always secure with us. Anytime you want, see you history with your customers. Moreover, you also have the option to print your builti forms at the end of a delivery.

Communicate with your client.

Communicate seamlessly with your client and make sure the two of you are always on the same page regarding the payment, pickup locations and destinations.
